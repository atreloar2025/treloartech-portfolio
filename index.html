About Me

<<<<<<< HEAD

I am a second-year master's of communication student at New Mexico State University. I spent the end of my undergrad here while playing baseball for the school. I now get to coach baseball at the school as our director of pitching analytics. Baseball has always been a passion of mine. It has been wonderful to be able to share my knowledge of the game, to up and coming collegiate baseball players.

Credentials

Rapsodo Certified, Driveline Certified, Director of Pitching Analytics for 2 years at New Mexico State, Pitching Coach for 2 years with Arroyo Seco Saints, a collegiate summer team in Pasadena, California that competes in the prestigious California Collegiate League. To go along with coaching, I have 100 weekend innings of leverage relief at the division 1 level and summer baseball levels. In high school, I was voted All-League, All-Region, All-Area, and All-District as both a junior and a senior. Played at division 2, JUCO, and division 1 levels over 6 years of college baseball.
